Lux Note

The Lux Note is a AAA-rated US Treasury collateralized investment note, ingeniously structured to generate significant returns without compromising the security of the investment principal. This innovative and time-proven financial instrument brings together the reliability of traditional investment notes with the versatility of modern financial structures.
Preserve Principal

keyboard_arrow_down

Highly liquid

Lux Note investments are secure, diversified, and come with a clearly defined risk profile controlled by each individual investor. These notes can be hypothecated, offering recognized value and liquidity, and providing multiple exit opportunities. The Lux Note is designed to cater to the needs of the savvy investor seeking high liquidity and flexibility, without compromising on security or potential returns. Invest with confidence, knowing your assets are backed by the solidity of US Treasury collateral and the innovative strategies of Lux.

keyboard_arrow_down

Principal protected

Every Lux Note is designed with a robust protection mechanism for your principal. Each note is equipped with two separable European Style Liquidating Options (ESLOs), one assigned to the principal and the other to the interest. The ESLO allocated to the principal ensures the full value of the principal is protected. Simultaneously, the ESLO allocated to the interest captures the net present value of future cashflows that are attributable to the underlying coupon payments. This dual ESLO system provides an additional layer of security, safeguarding both your principal and interest earnings, and reinforcing our commitment to a secure and risk-controlled investment environment.
